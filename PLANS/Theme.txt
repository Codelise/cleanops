Design System:
- Primary Color: #228B22 (Forest Green)
- Secondary Color: #0056B3 (Trust Blue)
- Accent Color: #FF8C00 (Action Orange)
- Neutral Colors: #F5F5F5 (Light BG), #333333 (Dark Text)
- Fonts: Poppins for headings (600 weight), Inter for body (400 weight)
- Spacing: 8px base unit (8, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64)
- Border Radius: 8px
- Shadows: Subtle elevation (0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.1))
